HOW TO PLAY

  • Every room contains 3 puzzles.
  • Every puzzle has a related activity.
  • Wrong answers decrease health by 20.
  • If the timer reaches zero, the room restarts.
  • If health reaches zero, the game restarts.
  • The Scientist can use hint popups.
  • Complete 3 puzzles to unlock the next room.
  • Complete all rooms to escape the facility.
  • Your progress is saved automatically by the C++ server!


ABOUT THE GAME

You awaken with no memory in a highly secured, abandoned underground facility. To survive and escape, you must navigate through 5 distinct rooms. Each room contains 3 challenging puzzles and 3 corresponding activities. Prove your intelligence, solve the mysteries, and find your way out before time runs out.

⚙️ Powered by a C++ backend server that reads questions from text files and saves your progress persistently across sessions.
